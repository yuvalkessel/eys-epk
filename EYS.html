About

Formed in London in late 2024, EYS is a psychedelic rock power trio channeling the raw spirit of classic rock with a heady, modern twist. Drawing influence from the likes of Led Zeppelin, Black Sabbath, and Cream, their sound is a swirling blend of fuzz-drenched riffs, hypnotic grooves, and mind-bending improvisation.


Known for their electrifying and unpredictable live shows, they've quickly built a following with performances that feel more like ritual than concert – loud, wild, and unapologetically unhinged.
